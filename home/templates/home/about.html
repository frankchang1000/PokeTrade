About

PokeTrade is a dynamic online platform where players can build, manage, and expand their Pokémon collections through trading and purchasing. After registering for an account, new players are welcomed with a random set of starter Pokémon to kickstart their journey. The home page provides a clear overview of the platform’s purpose and features.

Whether you're a casual collector or a dedicated trader, PokeTrade offers a streamlined and engaging environment to connect with other Pokémon enthusiasts and grow your ultimate team.
